## 📌 Project Overview

**Graduate Skill Index - Career Accelerator** is a responsive and modern landing page project created to highlight the importance of skill development among graduates in India.

Many students complete their degrees but struggle to find suitable job opportunities due to a lack of practical skills, industry exposure, and career guidance.

This project presents a platform concept that helps students become **industry-ready professionals** by focusing on:

- Practical skill development
- Real-world project experience
- Interview preparation
- Career mentorship

The goal is to encourage students to move beyond theoretical education and build skills required by modern industries.
